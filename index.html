About Us

Your Story, Our Masterpiece ✨💎

Sankar Jewellers is one of Kolkata’s leading destinations for fine jewellery, established in the late 1980s by Mr. Sankar Kumar Das. We are renowned for crafting high-quality, handcrafted pieces that blend contemporary style with timeless cultural heritage, catering to both modern women and men. With our own in-house manufacturing unit, we ensure superior craftsmanship and strict quality control.


Our services include gold and silver jewellery repair, plating, and custom jewellery design tailored to individual preferences. We also offer exquisite diamond jewellery certified by IGI and other reputed authorities. Our mission is to deliver luxury, elegance, and trust through complete transparency in pricing. We invite you to visit our Chetla showroom and explore an exclusive collection of gold, silver, diamond, and custom-made jewellery designed to be cherished for generations.


Located in the heart of Kolkata, Sankar Jewellers is dedicated to bringing you the finest gold and silver jewellery. We take pride in our 'Purity First' philosophy, ensuring that every gram of gold and every gemstone is ethically sourced and certified. Our master artisans combine age-old techniques with modern design to create jewellery that doesn't just look beautiful, but tells a story of heritage and luxury. Whether it’s a daily-wear piece or a wedding masterpiece, we craft it with the same level of devotion.


At Sankar Jewellers, we believe that jewellery is more than just an accessory—it is a celebration of life’s most precious moments. For generations, we have been a trusted name in Kolkata, crafting timeless pieces that blend traditional Bengali artistry with contemporary elegance. From the intricate gold work of our bridal collections to the modern shimmer of our silver and platinum sets, every piece is a testament to our commitment to purity, precision, and the art of fine jewellery making.
